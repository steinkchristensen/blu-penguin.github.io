About Me

Professional

I am a senior at Colorado State University pursuing dual degrees in Computer Engineering and Computer Science, and I have completed a mathematics minor. I have experience as a software engineer intern at both Ricoh in Boulder, CO and Bounce Software in Fort Collins, CO.

I am fascinated by innovations in science and technology, with career interests in aerospace, robotics, artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, and brain-computer interfaces. I would like to work in industry for a few years before returning to school to pursue a Masters or PhD degree. The rapid growth in technology in recent years has been made possible by advancements in computer technologies, so I plan to use the skills I have learned at CSU to continue the advancement of this field and positively impact the lives of millions of people across the globe. Specifically, I hope to contribute to space exploration, development of prosthetic limbs, or computer vision research.
